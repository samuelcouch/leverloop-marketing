## Feature Pages (`/features/[slug]`)

### 1. Speed-to-Lead Engine (`/features/speed-to-lead-engine`)
- **Badge:** ⚡ Speed-to-Lead
- **Headline:** "The first company to respond wins. Make it yours every time."
- **Description:** Auto-reply to every new lead in under 60 seconds, then keep follow-up moving until they book, reply, or opt out.
- **Detail 1 — Instant Response:** "Reply to every lead before your competitor does." Personalized business text sent the second a lead enters. Benefit: Protect ad spend by converting inquiries while intent is highest.
- **Detail 2 — Automated Follow-Up:** "Keep conversations alive without dispatcher effort." Drip sequence continues automatically until clear outcome. Benefit: No warm leads lost in inbox backlog.
- **Detail 3 — Pipeline Visibility:** "Track response outcomes across your whole funnel." Stage updates, replies, booking actions logged in real time. Benefit: Speed and accountability in one workflow.
- **FAQs:** Customizable first-response message; works after hours with quiet-hours compliance.

### 2. Lead Pipeline Visual Board (`/features/lead-pipeline-visual-board`)
- **Badge:** 📊 Lead Pipeline
- **Headline:** "See every opportunity at a glance so nothing slips."
- **Description:** Visual board from New to Won with timestamps, aging indicators, real-time team updates.
- **Detail 1 — Stage Clarity:** Clear operational stages; one shared source of pipeline truth.
- **Detail 2 — One-Click Conversion:** Turn Won leads into jobs without double entry. Sales handoff to operations is fast and clean.
- **FAQs:** Filter by rep/job type; stage transitions trigger automations.

### 3. Customer Database (`/features/customer-database`)
- **Badge:** 🗂️ Customer Records
- **Headline:** "Every customer, property, and service record in seconds."
- **Description:** Search by name, phone, address, or equipment; full relationship history instantly.
- **Detail 1 — Unified Profile:** Timeline view combines jobs, estimates, invoices, notes, photos, communications. Dispatchers answer without searching multiple systems.
- **Detail 2 — Compliance Built-In:** Opt-in source, timestamp, messaging status captured for audit-ready records.
- **FAQs:** Techs access history before arriving via crew work order URL; search by serial number or address.

### 4. Equipment & Asset Tracking (`/features/equipment-asset-tracking`)
- **Badge:** 🏷️ Asset Tracking
- **Headline:** "Turn every service call into data you can sell from."
- **Description:** Log make, model, serial, install date, photos, service history for every unit at every property.
- **Detail 1 — Fast Capture:** Create equipment records in under two minutes from the field with data plate photos.
- **Detail 2 — Lifecycle History:** Completed jobs build service history per asset for diagnostics and recommendations.
- **FAQs:** Asset history remains through property ownership changes.

### 5. Estimate Builder — Good / Better / Best (`/features/estimate-builder-good-better-best`)
- **Badge:** 🧾 Good / Better / Best
- **Headline:** "Send option-based proposals from the field in minutes."
- **Description:** Clear choices on customer's phone, digital approval, one-tap conversion to invoice.
- **Detail 1 — Three-Tier Quotes:** Pre-configured service bundles for Good/Better/Best. Customers choose value levels instead of rejecting a single number.
- **Detail 2 — Phone-First Experience:** Proposal link by text; compare tiers, sign, approve without app downloads or PDFs. Approvals while urgency is highest.
- **Detail 3 — Fast Handoff:** Approved tier details flow into operations and billing automatically. Faster fulfillment, fewer data errors.
- **FAQs:** Inexperienced techs can quote confidently; can mark one option as recommended.

### 6. Flat-Rate Pricebook (`/features/flat-rate-pricebook`)
- **Badge:** 💵 Flat-Rate Pricing
- **Headline:** "Consistent, profitable pricing in every tech's pocket."
- **Description:** Standardize service pricing by trade; field quotes aligned with target margins.
- **Detail 1 — Owner Control:** Admins set margins; techs search and select but cannot alter approved rates. No margin leakage.
- **FAQs:** Customize prices by local market; disable seasonal/outdated services.

### 7. Digital Approvals & Signatures (`/features/digital-approvals-signatures`)
- **Badge:** ✍️ Digital Signatures
- **Headline:** "Approve estimates and completed work from any phone."
- **Description:** Legally auditable signatures in seconds; no paper, scans, or follow-up chasing.
- **Detail 1 — Mobile Approval:** Tokenized link, review terms, draw signature, approve in under 90 seconds.
- **Detail 2 — Legal Trail:** Signed records include selection details, IP metadata, timestamp history.
- **Detail 3 — Workflow Trigger:** Approvals notify office; trigger downstream job or invoice actions automatically.
- **FAQs:** No account needed to sign; links are time-limited and resendable.

### 8. Invoicing & Text-to-Pay (`/features/invoicing-text-to-pay`)
- **Badge:** 💳 Text-to-Pay
- **Headline:** "Send an invoice by text and get paid before the truck leaves."
- **Description:** Turn approved work into secure payment links; card, ACH, partial payment support.
- **Detail 1 — One-Tap Conversion:** Line items, taxes, customer details flow into invoice drafts automatically.
- **Detail 2 — Secure Collection:** Customers pay by card or ACH through secure branded link via Stripe.
- **Detail 3 — Real-Time Confirmation:** Webhook updates mark jobs paid, issue receipts, keep accounting aligned.
- **FAQs:** Deposits/partial payments supported; settlement follows Stripe payout schedule (1–2 business days).

### 9. Dispatch Board (`/features/dispatch-board`)
- **Badge:** 📅 Dispatch
- **Headline:** "Run your entire service day from one calendar view."
- **Description:** See every job and open slot in real time; keep crew moving and customers informed.
- **Detail 1 — Calendar Scheduling:** Assign jobs, manage team's day from one view with crew assignments and time slots.
- **Detail 2 — Live Status:** Status updates from crew work order URLs feed board automatically including on-my-way and completion events.
- **FAQs:** Board is responsive for mobile dispatchers.

### 10. On My Way Notifications (`/features/on-my-way-notifications`)
- **Badge:** 📲 ETA Texts
- **Headline:** "One tap tells customers who is coming and when."
- **Description:** Automatic on-my-way messages with crew name and ETA; reduce inbound status calls.
- **Detail 1 — One-Tap Send:** Crew triggers from work order URL without calling office.
- **Detail 2 — Branded Context:** Messages include crew member name and arrival window.
- **Detail 3 — Delivery Logging:** Delivery status and send history visible to office staff.
- **FAQs:** ETA adjustable by technician; dispatch sees if message was sent.

### 11. Drip Campaigns (`/features/drip-campaigns`)
- **Badge:** 💬 Drip Campaigns
- **Headline:** "Lifecycle messaging that runs while your team runs jobs."
- **Description:** Prebuilt trade-specific sequences across lead nurture, estimate follow-up, reactivation, renewals.
- **Detail 1 — Trigger-Based Entry:** Campaigns start from new leads, sent estimates, completed jobs, inactivity, membership milestones.
- **Detail 2 — Trade-Specific Templates:** Prebuilt messaging; fast starting point, customizable to match brand voice.
- **Detail 3 — Exit Conditions:** Replies, approvals, bookings automatically remove customers from active sequences.
- **FAQs:** Custom multi-step workflows supported; dynamic merge fields (name, company, technician).

### 12. Unified Inbox (`/features/unified-inbox`)
- **Badge:** 📥 Unified Inbox
- **Headline:** "See every customer text and email in one thread."
- **Description:** Centralize human and automated communication; dispatchers always have context before replying.
- **Detail 1 — Single Timeline:** Outbound reminders, lead texts, dispatcher replies threaded by customer.
- **Detail 2 — Real-Time Routing:** Inbound message events post directly, flag unread state for quick action.
- **Detail 3 — Search & Status:** Search by contact, phone, message content; opt-in context visible.
- **FAQs:** Metadata indicates automated vs. human sends; replies use business number, not personal device.

### 13. Photo & Job Documentation (`/features/photo-job-documentation`)
- **Badge:** 📸 Job Documentation
- **Headline:** "Capture proof of work while the job is happening."
- **Description:** Before/after photos, data plates, notes stored with every job record.
- **Detail 1 — In-App Capture:** Take and tag photos directly in job workflow; no images trapped on personal camera rolls.
- **Detail 2 — Permanent History:** Documentation propagates into long-term customer history for future visits.
- **Detail 3 — Risk Protection:** Timestamped visual proof for quality and damage disputes.
- **FAQs:** Photos taggable by type (before, after, data plate, issue, other); uploads appear in near real time.

### 14. Team Management & Invitations (`/features/team-management-invitations`)
- **Badge:** 👥 Team Management
- **Headline:** "Invite, onboard, and offboard team members in minutes."
- **Description:** Role-based invites by email; speed up new-hire setup; disable access instantly.
- **Detail 1 — Fast Invites:** Add new users with email and role in one step. Day-one productivity without IT bottlenecks.
- **Detail 2 — Lifecycle Control:** Resend, revoke, or disable access anytime. Reduced security risk from stale accounts.
- **Detail 3 — Role Landing:** New members land in right workspace with correct tools from first login.
- **FAQs:** Pending invites revocable; role changes apply immediately without re-login.

### 15. Role-Based Access (`/features/role-based-access`)
- **Badge:** 🔐 Access Control
- **Headline:** "Show each role exactly what they need, nothing more."
- **Description:** Protect sensitive data; keep field and office workflows simple and focused.
- **Detail 1 — Role Design:** Owner and admin roles reflect operational needs, avoid permission sprawl.
- **Detail 2 — Server Enforcement:** Authorization enforced at backend, not just UI-level. Secure against direct URL attempts.
- **Detail 3 — Instant Updates:** Role updates propagate quickly during hiring and reorganization.
- **FAQs:** Technicians cannot access financial dashboards; permissions enforced server-side.

### 16. Flat Monthly Pricing (`/features/flat-monthly-pricing`)
- **Badge:** 🧮 Flat Pricing
- **Headline:** "One monthly price for your whole team. No per-seat tax."
- **Description:** Add dispatchers, techs, managers without pricing penalties; adoption scales with business.
- **Detail 1 — Growth-Aligned:** Flat pricing removes per-seat friction; onboard full team by default.
- **Detail 2 — Full-Team Adoption:** Stronger data when everyone uses the same system.
- **Detail 3 — Predictable Spend:** Stable software costs; avoids surprise invoices tied to hiring cycles.
- **FAQs:** No pricing change when adding technicians; seasonal staff onboarded without plan changes.

### 17. Smart Delivery (`/features/smart-delivery`)
- **Badge:** 📬 Smart Delivery
- **Headline:** "Reliable, compliant delivery for every business message."
- **Description:** Route SMS/email through provider-agnostic infrastructure with failover, audit logs, compliance controls.
- **Detail 1 — Automatic Failover:** Route around provider outages without disruption. Critical workflows like speed-to-lead keep firing.
- **Detail 2 — Compliance Guardrails:** Enforce opt-ins, quiet hours, stop handling. Opt-out updates applied immediately.
- **Detail 3 — Audit Visibility:** Delivery metadata, provider logs, event timestamps create complete communication trail.
- **FAQs:** Failover matters because lead response/ETA/payment reminders are operationally critical; STOP/opt-out processed immediately.
